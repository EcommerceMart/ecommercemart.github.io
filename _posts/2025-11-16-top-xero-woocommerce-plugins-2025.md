### Key Features

* Real-time order syncing
* Automatic product + inventory sync
* Customer mapping and payment method mapping
* Tax, shipping, and fee syncing
* Supports large stores

### Pros

* Excellent automation
* Best for high-volume stores
* Fast syncing

### Cons

* Higher pricing
* Requires initial setup time

Premium monthly subscription based on order volume.

Businesses needing **deep WooCommerce-to-Xero automation**.

## How to Set Up Xero WooCommerce Login

To connect WooCommerce with Xero:

1. Login to your **Xero account**
2. Go to **Settings → Connected Apps**
3. Authorize the WooCommerce plugin you installed
4. Configure:

   * Tax mapping
   * Payment gateway rules
   * Order mapping
5. Test with a sample order
