## Why Sizing Accuracy Is Crucial

When shopping online, especially for body-conscious items like **shapewear** or **shaping dresses**, precision in fit becomes essential.

### Why it matters:
- Avoid discomfort from compression or stretching
- Improve posture and confidence
- Cut down on returns with the correct size the first time

## How to Choose Your Right Size

To shop correctly with Popilush Models in mind:
