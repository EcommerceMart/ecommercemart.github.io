## 1. Website Overview and Domain Details

One of the first indicators of a site’s legitimacy is its **domain registration and transparency**.

* **Domain Age:** Newer domains (less than a year old) can be a red flag. As of 2025, Fashionica’s domain appears to have been registered recently, which may raise caution.
* **Contact Information:** Legitimate online retailers typically list detailed contact options, including a phone number, business address, and email. Fashionica’s website provides only limited contact information, which makes it harder for consumers to reach support directly.
* **SSL Certificate:** The site uses HTTPS, which means customer data (like credit card details) is encrypted — a positive sign.
